About Us

  1. 🌱

    Question 1: What is Infinite Sprouts?

    Infinite Sprouts is a visionary ecosystem powered by AI, IoT, and Blockchain, aiming to expand the scale and consciousness of agriculture through its seven-point agenda. It is a unified ecosystem for everyone.

  2. 🌱

    Question 2: What is the vision?

    We envision a future where agriculture runs seamlessly on Blockchain, creating an ecosystem that provides solutions for farmers and an efficient investment platform for investors. This allows anyone, anywhere, to contribute to and benefit from agriculture through our staking protocol.

  3. 🌱

    Question 3: What is the goal?

    Our goal is to ensure the long-term survival of humanity on Earth by supporting farmers to scale and address daily challenges in their farming activities. We also aim to enable anyone, anywhere, to invest in or contribute to agriculture and benefit from it.

  4. 🌱

    Question 4: What is the mission?

    Our mission is to build a decentralized platform that helps farmers grow and enables anyone to contribute-tailored to and benefit from agriculture.

Our Ecosystem

Sproutlink

A global network connecting farmers worldwide to share insights and grow together in a digital, free, fair, and unified ecosystem.

Sprout Farm

Sprout Farm is our agenda to engage in large-scale farming of ginger, rice, corn, palm oil, and cocoa, generating significant revenue for our ecosystem.

Sprouts Exchange

Sprouts Exchange is one of our agendas that brings farmers into the new age, allowing them to participate in the Web3 economy.

SproutsLabs

A hub for development and innovation, SproutsLabs is a protocol that insists every farmer is heard and attended to effectively. With SproutsLabs, farmers can report sudden problems on their farms and receive faster responses.

SproutStake

An investment protocol where users stake tokens to earn passive income from our ecosystem, with revenue from farming, storage, lending to farmers, and the platform.

Sprouts Bank

A financial platform offering tailored services like loans and savings for agriculture.

Sprouts Wallet

A user-friendly wallet for managing crop tokens, staking rewards, and transactions.
